About us

Our mission

We are committed to providing high quality healthcare services, promoting the health and well-being of our patients. Our mission is to be a leader in healthcare by combining professionalism, innovation and humanity.

Our history

With major additions to our staff, the opening of new departments and the introduction of state-of-the-art technology, we continue to grow and evolve to better meet the needs of our patients. Since our founding in 1990, our hospital has grown into one of the leaders in healthcare services.

Our team

Our physicians and medical staff are experienced professionals with a high level of professionalism and patient care. We strive to create a friendly and supportive atmosphere for our employees, as we believe that happy employees make for better outcomes for our patients.
